Questions

Good to know

Is my data really private?

Yes. Your logs, cravings and Room conversations live only on your phone — they're never uploaded. Only your account and subscription are stored in the cloud, so you can sign in on a new device.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Tracking, stats, ranks and the breathing timer all work with no connection. Only the live AI chat needs internet.

Which languages are supported?

English, Bahasa Melayu, Arabic and French — across the whole app, including the AI coach, which replies in your chosen language.

What does it cost?

The core app is free forever. Premium ($3.99/month) unlocks the Craving SOS toolkit, the live AI coach and The Room. Cancel anytime.

Does it work for vaping too?

Yes — cigarettes, vaping, rolled tobacco and shisha. The stats and savings adapt to what you use.
